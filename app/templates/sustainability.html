Eco-Scores Explained

Eco-Scores rate environmental impact from A (best) to E (worst), considering carbon footprint, packaging, and production methods.

Sustainable Packaging

Look for recyclable, biodegradable, or minimal packaging. Reusable containers are the most eco-friendly option.

Local vs Global

Local products typically have lower carbon footprints due to reduced transportation distances and fresher ingredients.

How Sustainability Scores Are Calculated

Our sustainability scoring system evaluates products across multiple environmental factors to provide you with transparent and comprehensive eco-ratings.

Scoring Methodology

Multi-Factor Assessment

Each product is evaluated across 4 core environmental dimensions

Weighted Scoring

Factors are weighted based on their environmental significance

Grade Assignment

Final scores are converted to letter grades A through E

Score Distribution

A Excellent (80-100 points) Top 25%
B Good (60-79 points) Next 35%
C Fair (40-59 points) Middle 25%
D Poor (20-39 points) Bottom 10%
E Bad (0-19 points) Bottom 5%
